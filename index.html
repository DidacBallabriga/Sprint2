A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favorite websites. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load instantly. Try it for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favorite websites, Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can acces them on de go.

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favourite sites.

Speedy Searchoing

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time at all. No need to trawl through all of you bookmarks.

Easy Sharing

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with others. Create a shareable link that you can send at the click of a button
